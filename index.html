TakaSmart Bin Project

Despite all the efforts by the government and private sectors to manage solid waste, heaps of garbage continues to pile up in dumpsites and landfills in Nairobi. The effects of lack of proper solid waste management strategies can be felt first hand by communities living around the dumpsites. The dumpsites pose both health and environmental risks to the people living around and ore, especially to children. The project aimed to come up with a solution that would help increase waste recycle therefore less waste ending up in dumpsites. In a group of three, my role was to design the bin in Fusion 360 and create a dashboard to help monitor bins sold out..

IBM SmartWaterChallenge

Water is one of the most precious resources on the planet. 50% of the world’s cities experience water shortages on a recurring basis Continuous growth in population and climate changes around the world aggravates the problem beyond the water sector can address. IBM created a challenge to attract innovators to create effective, creative, and sustainable solutions that would have a lasting, positive, and significant impact in addressing water issues. As a finalist participant, alongside three other team members, we used Machine Learning, IoT and Cloud computing to come up with a solution to help create transparency in the water management system.
